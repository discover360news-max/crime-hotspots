Elderly family held hostage: armed trio raids Santa Cruz home.
At approximately 12:55 p.m. on Wednesday, 10th December, 2025, two women, aged 72 and 77, were at their Cangrejal Road, Lower Santa Cruz residence with their 102-year-old mother and the mother’s two caregivers when three men—one armed with a firearm—forced their way inside.
The suspects announced a robbery and bound the hands of all the victims, except the elderly mother, using tie straps. They then ransacked the home while demanding cash and jewellery.
During the ordeal, the 72-year-old woman was robbed of $2,500 in cash and several pieces of gold jewellery. The two caregivers were also relieved of their cellphones.
After the robbery, the trio calmly walked out of the house and escaped.
The victims eventually managed to free themselves and alerted the police, who have since launched an investigation.

Between 10:00 pm on Tuesday 10th December, 2025, and 2:00 am on Wednesday 11th December, 2025, officers of the Central Division Task Force, Areas North and South, conducted an Operation Festive Shield exercise.
