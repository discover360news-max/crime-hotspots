## Privacy and Your Report

This article explains how Crime Hotspots handles data submitted through the Report a Crime and Submit a Safety Tip forms.

## What we collect

When you submit a report or tip, we collect only:

- The information you enter in the form fields
- A CAPTCHA token (Cloudflare Turnstile) to prevent automated spam — this does not identify you

We do not require an account, email address, or any identifying information to submit. If you do not provide contact details, your submission is fully anonymous on our end.

## What we publish

We never publish:

- Your name, contact information, or any details that could identify you as the submitter
- Full names of victims or witnesses without their consent (we use first names or initials only, matching how the original media source reported the incident)
- Full names of suspects who have not been officially charged or named by police

What we do publish:

- Verified incident details: crime type, date, area, brief description
- A link back to the original media source (if one exists)

## How long we retain data

Unverified or rejected reports are retained in our internal database for 90 days for reference, then deleted. Verified, published incidents are retained indefinitely as part of the public record.

## Third-party tools

The Report a Crime form uses **Cloudflare Turnstile** for spam prevention. Turnstile does not set cookies and does not track users across sites. See [Cloudflare's privacy policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/) for details.

## Your rights

You may contact us to:

- Request removal of a specific published record
- Correct inaccurate details in a published record
- Ask what information we hold from a submission

Contact us via the [Contact page](/contact/). We respond to data-related requests within 5 business days.

## Children

We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 16. If you believe a submission was made by a minor, contact us immediately.
