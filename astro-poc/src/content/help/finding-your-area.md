## Finding Your Area

Crime Hotspots covers hundreds of areas across Trinidad & Tobago. Each area has its own profile showing recent incidents, crime breakdowns, and a risk score.

## Using the search bar

The quickest way to find your area is the search bar at the top of any page (or press `Ctrl+K` / `Cmd+K`). Type the name of your neighbourhood, community, or district. Results appear instantly — tap an area to go to its profile.

## Browsing the Areas page

Go to [Trinidad Areas](/trinidad/areas/) to see all covered neighbourhoods organised by region. You can:

- Browse by region (e.g. East, West, Central, South, Tobago)
- See the risk score and incident count for each area at a glance
- Click any area card to open the full area profile

## What if my area is not listed?

Coverage depends on reported incidents. If an area has had no recorded incidents in our dataset, it may not appear as a standalone page. You can:

1. Check the region page — your area may be grouped under a broader region
2. Search using alternative names or spellings
3. [Contact us](/contact/) to suggest an area for inclusion

## Reading an area profile

Each area profile shows:

- **Risk score** (1–10): relative crime density over the past 90 days
- **Recent incidents**: the latest verified crime reports for that area
- **Crime type breakdown**: which types of offences are most common
- **Safety tips**: context-specific advice based on the area's crime profile
- **Nearby MPs**: elected representatives for that constituency
