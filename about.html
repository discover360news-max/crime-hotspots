About Crime Hotspots

Our Mission

Crime Hotspots is a regional initiative designed to create greater awareness and provide accessible, data-driven insights into serious crimes across the Caribbean. Our goal is to help communities, journalists, and policymakers better understand crime patterns through verified reporting and transparent data aggregation.

How We Collect Data

All statistics presented on this platform are compiled from verified media reports, police statements, and open-source data. Each incident is cross-referenced with reputable news sources before inclusion to maintain accuracy and credibility.

Location markers shown on maps represent estimated positions based on available information. While the Area and Street Address fields reflect accurate data from verified reports, map coordinates may be approximate and are not intended for investigative use.

Our Purpose

The purpose of Crime Hotspots is to create an open and reliable avenue for citizens and researchers to view up-to-date statistics on serious crimes across the Caribbean. By centralizing data in one platform, we aim to promote public awareness, support journalism, and contribute to community safety efforts throughout the region.

Data Transparency

Our data is updated regularly as new reports are verified. Updates are made within 24 to 48 hours of confirmed publication in recognized media outlets. We are committed to maintaining accuracy and openness in how we source, clean, and publish our data.

If you notice an error, missing detail, or have verified information to share, please contact us directly at discover360news@gmail.com. We welcome collaboration and feedback that improves the reliability of this public resource.
